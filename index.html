Prep Work

  1. Get fresh baby back ribs
  2. Wash and dry off ribs
  3. Pull silver skin off with paper towel
  4. Season with rib rub generously
  5. Cover with plastic wrap and store in fridge overnight

Rib rub mix

  • 1 Tbs cumin
  • 1 Tbs paprika
  • 1 Tbs granulated garlic
  • 1 Tbs granulated onion
  • 1 Tbs chili power
  • 1 Tbs brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs kosher salt
  • 1 Tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 Tsp black pepper
  • 1 Tsp white pepper
